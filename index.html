About me

I'm Muhammad Sharyar Bin Zafar, a Unity game developer who's been building mobile games for the past few years. My journey into game development started from a young age - I was a huge fan of GTA Vice City and spent countless hours playing it. I was always curious about how games are made, which questions sparked my interest in game development. This childhood passion eventually led me to pursue a career in creating games myself.

I completed my BS-IT from University of Okara, which gave me a solid foundation in programming and software development. What started as a hobby quickly turned into a passion for creating games that people actually enjoy playing.

My focus is on developing complete games from start to finish - from initial concept to final product. I work with Unity, C#, and integrate various SDKs for analytics, ads, and monetization. Most of my work involves simulation games, puzzle games, and hyper-casual titles.

When I'm not coding, I'm usually thinking about new game mechanics or ways to improve player experience. I believe in creating games that are not just fun, but also polished and well-optimized for mobile devices. Every project teaches me something new, and that's what keeps me motivated.
